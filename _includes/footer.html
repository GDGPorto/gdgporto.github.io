#GDGPorto

We think that Porto as other great European cities has a growing number of startups, and we can be a big if not the biggest tech hub in Portugal.

Having this in mind we though that something's missing in Porto that already exists on cities like London or Paris. A community of developers. We want to make it possible for the community to spread the knowledge between people in different companies, schools and universities. We want to be that bridge.
