GDG Porto Work

To help our developer community to grow, we have some interest events, some of them are:

Google DevFest

Large annual conference where we invite national and international speakers. Sharing and discussion hub about Google technology's. The event has one or more days, more than 100 participants and it's similar to the one organized by Google.

Android Sessions

The goal of this event is to gather the Android community in Porto, helping everyone to get up to date and to know Android a little bit better. We'll share knowledge and experience about the Android ecosystem.

Google Hashcode

Hash Code is a team-based programming competition organized by Google for students and industry professionals across Europe, the Middle East and Africa. You pick your team and programming language, Google picks a real-life engineering problem for you to solve.

Google Roadshow

Googlers cross Portugal giving talks and codelabs about Progressive Web Apps, Firebase and Android.
